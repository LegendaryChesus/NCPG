Law

Residents adhere to a strict code of honesty, refraining from any form of theft or dishonest practices. 5. **Noise Curfew:** After 11 PM, a serene calm envelops the community as a noise curfew takes effect. This ensures peaceful nights for everyone to rest and rejuvenate. 6. **Respect for All:** Respect is the cornerstone of interactions within the community.
